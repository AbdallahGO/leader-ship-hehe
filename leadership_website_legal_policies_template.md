**A:** The Apex Leadership Summit 2026 takes place live on August 12–13, 2026. The Studio Audience experience is hosted at Willow Creek Community Church in South Barrington, Illinois. Local host sites span 100+ countries and 400+ venues globally.

---

## Q: What's included with my ticket?

**A:** All tickets include access to every public session, speaker talks, and artistic elements of the Summit. Studio Audience tickets additionally include live on-stage access, book signings, exclusive merchandise, and networking events.

---

## Q: Do you offer group or team rates?

**A:** Yes! Bring a team of 5 and save $100+ with our Team Pass 5 at $179/ticket. Bring 6 or more and pay just $169/ticket with Team Pass Plus. Discounts are automatically applied and valid only for local Summit tickets.

---

## Q: Can I buy one ticket for my whole group?

**A:** No — each attendee requires an individual ticket for entry at host site venues and to access interactive elements of the online experience. You can register guests later if their details aren't available at the time of purchase.

---

## Q: What is the Executive Track?

**A:** The Executive Track is an exclusive Studio Audience upgrade at $1,999 that includes premium floor seating, catered lunches with select speakers, reserved parking, and access to a private executive lounge throughout the Summit. Space is limited.

---

## Q: What is the cancellation policy?

**A:** Full cancellation and transfer policies are available in our Terms & Conditions. Generally, cancellations made 30+ days before the event are eligible for a full refund. Transfers to another attendee are available up to 7 days prior.
