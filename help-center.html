Frequently Asked Questions

Can I register just one day instead of both days?
The full Summit experience spans both days (August 12–13). However, some local host sites may offer flexible options. Check with your local host site for details.
Are there student or military discounts?
Yes! Valid student and military IDs qualify for 15% off Local Summit tickets. Apply the discount code at checkout.
Will sessions be recorded?
Yes, all sessions are recorded. Online attendees receive on-demand access for 30 days after the event.
How can I get a refund?
Refunds are available for cancellations made 30+ days before the event. Process your refund through your account dashboard or contact support.
Is childcare available at the venue?
Childcare availability varies by location. Contact your local host site for information about children's services and pricing.
How do I contact support if I can't find my answer?
Visit our Contact Us page to reach our support team via email, phone, or live chat. We respond within 24 hours.
